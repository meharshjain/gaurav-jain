OUR PROCESS

Enjoy your green space and leave the maintenance worries to us. We are there to design, create and maintain your garden space. With our affordable maintenance package, you will have trained professionals visiting your home to ensure it remains lush & green always. Our maintenance package starts at only Rs.250 per month.

Our Design team will create customized designs based on your requirement and budget. We will provide you the 3D designs and details of the products to ensure complete transparency and satisfaction. We will ensure that the design reflects your taste.

We understand the value of delivering to our promises. Our assigned execution focal point will take over once the project is confirmed and will ensure hassle free, no intervention completion of your project. We prepare all the materials at our warehouse and then install it at one go to minimize your time commitment. For balcony gardens we commit a maximum 15 day installation time and for terrace gardens and landscaping we commit maximum up to 30 days.

Testimonial

Rajesh Prabhu

Bellandur

We contracted My Cloud Forest for converting a large part of our 1000 Sqft terrace. The purpose was to ‘green’ it up and break the visual and spatial monotony. We had already had the design and wanted someone who could bring it to life. It was a bit of a challenging project as many of the things we were trying to do were not standardised. MCF worked with us, took our designs and first worked out the technical details to finally come up with a near perfect solution to majority of our needs. The execution team was efficient and professional. The team have been very responsive and detail oriented through out the journey. The key to make this work is their technical capabilities, which is a must have in order to achieve a successful project. There are many outdoor contractors in the market who don't understand the technical challenges resulting in nightmarish problems further down the line.

The overall project has come up superbly. It was delivered on time with very good quality and the highly refined visual finish we were hoping for. And it has turned our dry terrace space into a green sanctuary and a very cool space for social gatherings and family meetups. A job wonderfully done by MCF.
